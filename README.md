# Player Performance Prediction & Optimal Team Builder

A player performance prediction tool developed for *OOTP 26*, designed to build the most competitive teams in the *Perfect Team* game mode.

---

## Background

This project began with my passion for *Out of the Park Baseball (OOTP)*, which I started playing back in 2018. I've always had an affinity for statistics and a competitive drive when it comes to both sports and games. At the time, I was 17 and just beginning my journey in coding—experimenting with small programs and websites.

It wasn’t until 2023, during my Computer Science degree at the University of Western Ontario, that I gained a deeper understanding of machine learning and its potential in predicting future outcomes and performance metrics. I began to wonder how I could apply these concepts to OOTP to create a strategic edge in gameplay.

In 2025, after completing my undergraduate degree, I enrolled in a machine learning certificate program at my local college. This project became a culmination of that journey. The goal was to reverse engineer which player ratings in OOTP were most predictive of success—to identify which players actually deliver the best results in-game.

---

## The Idea

In modern baseball analytics, **WAR (Wins Above Replacement)** is one of the most important metrics. It estimates how many wins a player contributes to their team compared to a replacement-level player, factoring in offense, defense, and baserunning. It’s often used to evaluate overall player value—those with the highest WAR often win MVP or Cy Young awards.

My goal was to use machine learning to analyze OOTP’s artificial player ratings—such as Contact, Power, Defense, Speed, etc.—and determine which ones most strongly influence WAR. This would allow me to identify underrated or overrated players and build an optimized team for competitive play in Perfect Team mode.
